I like math and problem solving -- and thus I often do math contests and propose problems to make them more challenging. Here's an example of a math problem:

Consider the following equation:

\( x^2 + y^2 = z^2 \)

Where \( x, y, z \) are integers, and the task is to find all the Pythagorean triples. This is a classic problem in number theory.

The solution can be derived using:

\( a = m^2 - n^2, \quad b = 2mn, \quad c = m^2 + n^2 \)

Where \( m \) and \( n \) are integers, and \( m > n \). This formula generates all primitive Pythagorean triples.
