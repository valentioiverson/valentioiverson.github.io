Math Contest

I like mathematics and problem solving a lot -- and that's probably reflected in my involvement in math contests throughout the years.

Teaching & Achievements

I have been actively involved with math contests for quite some time. Some of my notable past achievements include:

  • Bronze Medal, Iranian Geometry Olympiad 2018 and 2019
  • Absolute Winner, Indonesia National Math Olympiad 2019
  • Honorable Mention, Romanian Master of Mathematics 2020
  • Bronze Medal, Asia Pacific Mathematics Olympiad 2020
  • Bronze Medal, Tuymaada International Olympiad 2020
  • Second Prize, European Mathematical Cup 2020
  • Top 235 and 312 (7% and 8%), the William Lowell Putnam Mathematical Competition 2022 and 2023

I was also actively involved in KTO Matematika, an Indonesian nonprofit math olympiad community founded by Indonesian math olympiad alumni. I was the head of KTOM in 2020, and nowadays occasionally propose problems and perform quality checks.

Nowadays, I assist the Indonesian IMO team by teaching, grading, proposing problems, and doing quality checks for problem shortlists.

I am open to teaching, feel free to contact me for these type of inquiries.

Problem Proposals

I actively propose problems to math contests. Here is a selection of some of my past problem proposals:

IMO 2024/2 (N4)

Determine all pairs \( (a,b) \) of positive integers for which there exist positive integers \( g \) and \( N \) such that:

\[ \gcd(a^n + b, b^n + a) = g \]

holds for all integers \( n \ge N \).

Indonesia MO 2023/2
Determine all functions \( f: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R} \) such that the following equation holds for every real \( x,y \): \[ f(f(x) + y) = \lfloor x + f(f(y)) \rfloor \]
Indonesia MO 2023/8
Let \( a,b,c \) be three distinct positive integers. Define \( S(a,b,c) \) as the set of all rational roots of \( px^2 + qx + r = 0 \) for every permutation \( (p,q,r) \) of \( (a,b,c)\). For example, \( S(1,2,3) = \{ -1, -2, -1/2 \} \) because the equation \( x^2 + 3x + 2 \) has roots \( -1 \) and \( -2 \), the eequation \( 2x^2 + 3x + 1 = 0 \) has roots \( -1 \) and \( -1/2 \), and for all the other permutations of \( (1, 2, 3) \), the quadratic equations formed don't have any rational roots. Determine the maximum number of elements in \( S(a,b,c) \).
IMO Shortlist 2022 A6

Let \( \mathbb{R} \) be the set of real numbers. We denote by \( \mathcal{F} \) the set of all functions \( f: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R} \) such that:

\[ f(x+f(y)) = f(x) + f(y) \]

for every \( x, y \in \mathbb{R} \). Find all rational numbers \( q \) such that for every function \( f \in \mathcal{F} \), there exists some \( z \in \mathbb{R} \) satisfying \( f(z) = qz \).

APMO 2022/5
Let \( a,b,c,d \) be real numbers such that \( a^2 + b^2 + c^2 + d^2 = 1 \). Determine the minimum value of \( (a - b)(b - c)(c - d)(d - a) \) and determine all values of \( a,b,c,d \) such that the minimum value is achieved.
